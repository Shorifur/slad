Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chicken

Kala and quinoa provide a healthy base for roasdted chicken topped with a light Caesar salad

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Kale
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 1 Chicken breast
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast chicken until golden brown and 165 in middle.

    Time: 40 minutes

  2. Toss quinoa , Chicken , kale , and Caesar dessing until coated.

    Time :4 minutes

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish.
